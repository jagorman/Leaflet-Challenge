About Project


This project visualizes data from the United States Geological Survey (USGS). You are seeing all the recorded earthquakes in the past week, updated in real time.

The size of each circle reflects the magnitude of the earthquake. The color reflects the depth of the quake. The color scheme is rainbow, with lesser depths being on the purple end of the spectrum, greater depths being on the red end of the spectrum. Selecting a circle will provide you more information about the earthquake. A legend has been provided

In addition, tectonic outlines have been provided as well as user controlled overlays

The USGS is responsible for providing scientific data about natural hazards, the health of our ecosystems and environment; and the impacts of climate and land-use change. Their scientists develop new methods and tools to supply timely, relevant, and useful information about the Earth and its processes.
